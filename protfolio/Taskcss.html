Nature History

Nature is the encompassing world of physical and living things, from the smallest atom to the vast universe. It's a source of beauty, providing essential resources and shaping our planet's ecosystems. Nature includes diverse elements like mountains, oceans, forests, and the sky, as well as the living creatures that inhabit them
