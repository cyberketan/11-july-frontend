The Kedarnath Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is a revered Hindu shrine nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas. Its history is intertwined with both legend and reconstruction
According to legend, the temple was originally built by the Pandavas after the Mahabharata war to atone for their sins
The current structure, however, is attributed to Adi Shankaracharya, who is believed to have rebuilt it in the 8th century
